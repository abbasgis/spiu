SPIU & PGDP

PGDP is a World Bank funded program of worth US$ 273 Million. The share of World Bank and Government of the Punjab is US$ 200 Million and US$ 73 Million respectively. The key objectives of PGDP are:
      (a) Strengthening Environmental Governance &
      (b) Promoting Green Investments.

The SPIU at the EPD will have overall responsibility for monitoring and reporting on the Program’s results. It will conduct regular M&E reporting on progress in each of the results areas, including the collection of output milestones for intermediate indicators and Disbursement Link Indicators.

SPIU is being established for the World Bank funded “Punjab Green Development Program”, Asian Development Bank aided “Revitalization of River Ravi”, for planning and implementation of future interventions of Environment Protection Department to empower and support Environment Protection Department
