WHO WE ARE

The SPIU at the EPD will have overall responsibility for monitoring and reporting on the Program’s results. It will conduct regular M&E reporting on progress in each of the results areas, including the collection of output milestones for intermediate indicators and Disbursement Link Indicators.

SPIU is being established for the World Bank funded “Punjab Green Development Program”, Asian Development Bank aided “Revitalization of River Ravi”, for planning and implementation of future interventions of Environment Protection Department to empower and support Environment Protection Department in:

  • Looking into the future and therefore provide an opportunity to influence the future, or at least, assume a proactive posture.
  • Having better awareness of needs and of the facilities related issues and environment.
  • Defining the overall direction of environmental governance and focuses on the objectives.
  • Having a sense of direction, continuity, and effective staffing and leadership.
  • Plugging everyone into the system and provide standards of accountability for people, projects, and allocated resources

EDM

SPIU comprises of three Directorates i.e. (i) Environmental Policy & Planning (ii) Programmes & (iii) Admin Finance & Procurement. The directorate of Environmental Policy and Planning is inter alia responsible for Organizing and prioritize critical issues and required information in facilitating efficient decision making. Apart from carrying out other activities a spatial module for environmental efficient decision making for general public has been designed for selection of a site for establishment of poultry farms.

There are two main considerations for a site suitability regarding establishment of Poultry farms, one population count and second distance from other poultry farms. To get this done, data of administrative boundaries (Province, Division, District & Tehsil) of Punjab has been obtained from Urban Unit (Pvt.) Ltd. along with some other spatial layers. Population data is obtained from “World Pop”. This information has been used for generation of Spatial Database, gathered data from a few district officers of Environment. with in-house, limited capabilities, developed a web viewer for visualization of this data sets in line with the requirements for facilitating the general public in efficient decision making. A tool namely “Environmental Decision Making” abbreviated as EDM has been developed.
