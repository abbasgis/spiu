INTRODUCTION AND BACKGROUND

EPD’s SPIU (Strategic Planning & Implementing Unit) is mandated to execute and achieve different targets i.e. installation of Ambient Air Quality Monitoring Stations, Water Quality Monitoring Stations, Policy formulation on Sound Management of Plastics, Public disclosure of environmental information and citizen engagement and preparation of State of the Environment of Punjab Province. In this context, preparation of an inventory of those organizations which are collecting and analysing environmental data for informed decision making and to strategies the policy goals/ priority areas of intervention for environment protection in the Province has been initiated.

OBJECTIVE

Because environment is a cross cutting subject. Multiple organization including academia, research institutions and government departments etc. are playing their vital role for the betterment of the environment. The role of every organization is very important but limited to a certain aspect of the environment. The information – who is doing what – pertaining to the environmental governance, is not available in one Inventory. SPIU intends to draw a holistic picture of Who is Doing What and What Type of Environmental Data is available with other organizations/ government departments. This will further lead to devise corrective actions, projects and plans for better environmental monitoring.
